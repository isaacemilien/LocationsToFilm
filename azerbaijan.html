Azerbaijan

Azerbaijan is a new location for film industry, most of its unique pearls yet to be explored. With short distance between the cities and the countryside, all corners of the country are easily accessible. Rich History Azerbaijan is one of the rare countries where medieval architecture lives in peace with cutting edge designs, which can offer a backdrop to both historic and contemporary scenes.
